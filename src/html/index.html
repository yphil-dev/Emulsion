Welcome to Emulsion!

Emulsion is a game launcher that helps you organize and launch your games.

Setup

  • Select a platform on the home screen
  • Browse to your games directory
  • Enter your emulator for that platform
  • Enter your optional launch arguments for that emulator

Usage

  • Select a platform on the home screen
  • Browse your games and select one to launch
  • Use your keyboard or game controller to navigate

For more details, see the Keyboard Shortcuts tab or press any key to close.
